About Me

Hi there! I'm Betty, the creator behind MathMadeEasyBetty. My mission is simple: to make math clear, accessible, and even enjoyable for high school students. I break down tough concepts into easy-to-follow steps so you can build a strong foundation and feel confident in class and on exams.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, which gives me a deep understanding of how math is applied in the real world. From circuits to systems, I’ve seen firsthand how algebra, calculus, and statistics power technology. That’s why I focus not just on solving problems, but on showing how math connects to everyday life and future careers.

Over the years, I’ve guided thousands of students through Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, and beyond. Whether you’re preparing for a test or just need extra support, you’ll find lessons designed to help you succeed—one problem at a time.
